# Public communities — governance snapshot (Phase 2+)

**Purpose:** Until full community terms are counsel-approved, this is the **operating** description of how MBKRU community spaces relate to Voice, moderation, and member safety. **Not** a substitute for Terms of Use or community-specific rules shown in-product.

---

## 1. What a community is (on MBKRU)

- A **bounded space** (often geography- or institution-linked) for discussion, announcements, and moderated concerns.  
- **Not** a replacement for CHRAJ, police, courts, or employer whistleblower procedures.

### 1.1 Forums, threads, and replies

- **Forums** are named areas inside a community (for example “General discussion”). Operators seed a default forum where migrations apply; **moderators** or **Queen Mother verified** members may create additional forums.  
- A **thread** is a root-level post in a forum (optionally with a title). **Replies** attach to that thread; each reply is still a `CommunityPost` subject to the same moderation states.  
- **Locked forums** block new threads and replies from regular members until unlocked (moderators retain access where the product allows it).
- When a **reply is published** (immediately or after moderator approval), the **thread author** receives an in-app notification so they can return to the conversation — not a substitute for subscribing to every reply by email.
- **Forum thread lists** order pinned items first, then by **latest activity** on the root thread (publishing a reply bumps that thread for everyone browsing the forum). The “last activity” hint on the forum index uses **published** content only so pending moderation does not inflate the timestamp.

---

## 2. Moderation and verification

- **Moderators** act under programme rules; escalation paths must be documented per community where possible.  
- **Verification** (e.g. Queen Mother networks) is **programme identity**, not national ID proof in MVP — see admin community flows.  
- **Published posts** are the only posts that should appear in public search/browse; pending/rejected states stay staff-only.

---

## 3. Relationship to MBKRU Voice

- Voice reports are **individual** submissions with their own triage.  
- Community threads are **collective** signal. Staff may cross-reference internally; do not publish Voice narrative in community without explicit consent and editorial policy.
