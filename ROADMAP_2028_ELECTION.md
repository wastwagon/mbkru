# MBKRU Platform Roadmap — Ghana 2028 Election

**Purpose:** Realistic timeline and implementation plan aligned with Ghana's next general election (November–December 2028).  
**Last updated:** March 2026

## Executive Summary

MBKRU Advocates aims to position itself as Ghana's premier citizen accountability platform before the 2028 general election. This roadmap outlines phased implementation from Phase 1 (complete) through Phase 3 (election impact), with the **People's Report Card** and **Accountability Scorecards** (published 90 days before the election) as flagship deliverables.

**Key 2028 Milestone:** Accountability Scorecards published **August–September 2028** (90 days before election).

## How MBKRU Impacts the 2028 Election

### 1. People's Report Card (Pillar D)

- **What:** Annual data-driven assessment of every Minister, Regional Minister, and MP.
- **Impact:** By 2028, three report cards (2026, 2027, 2028) will exist. Voters can compare incumbents' performance against campaign promises.
- **Timeline:** Year 1 (2027), Year 2 (2028), Year 3 (2028 pre-election).

### 2. Accountability Scorecards (Pillar D)

- **What:** Published **90 days before** every general election. Pre-election debates and town halls in every constituency.
- **Impact:** Directly shapes voter information. Candidates and incumbents are held to their records. Media and CSOs use scorecards for debates and coverage.
- **Timeline:** Publication **August–September 2028**.

### 3. Campaign Promise Tracking (Pillar D)

- **What:** Real-time tracking of campaign promises versus delivery.
- **Impact:** 2024 promises become baseline. 2028 manifestos are tracked from day one. Voters see promise vs. delivery before casting ballots.
- **Timeline:** 2024 baseline by Q1 2027; 2028 manifestos from Q1 2028.

### 4. Physical Engagement (Pillar B)

- **What:** Quarterly Town Halls, Regional Forums, Annual National People's Assembly.
- **Impact:** Constituency-level debates and forums put accountability questions directly to candidates. National Assembly elevates citizen priorities to Presidency.
- **Timeline:** Constituency Town Halls in all 275 constituencies by Q3 2028.

### 5. MBKRU Voice (Pillar A)

- **What:** Digital platform for geo-tagged complaints, membership, and public statistics.
- **Impact:** Citizen-reported issues feed into People's Report Card and situational alerts. Demonstrates demand for accountability.
- **Timeline:** Full launch Q4 2026; scaled by 2028.

### 6. Legal Empowerment (Pillar C)

- **What:** CHRAJ, FOI, courts guidance. Pro-bono referrals. Voter rights support.
- **Impact:** Citizens can challenge electoral irregularities. FOI requests support data for scorecards. Legal support builds trust.
- **Timeline:** Pilot Q3 2026; full operations 2027.

### 7. Presidential Interface (Pillar E)

- **What:** Monthly Citizens' Brief, Quarterly Listening Sessions, liaison office.
- **Impact:** Citizen priorities reach Presidency. Demonstrates non-partisan bridge. Builds institutional credibility before election.
- **Timeline:** Liaison established 2027; Listening Sessions 2027–2028.
