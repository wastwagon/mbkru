## What Phase 1 Is NOT

- **No** user registration/login
- **No** complaint submission forms
- **No** geolocation or reporting workflows
- **No** MP/Minister data or scorecards
- **No** alert submission system

Those are **Phase 2** features. Phase 1 preview pages only *describe* and *tease* them.

## Enhancement Focus

Phase 1 enhancement = **design, UX, content, polish** — not new functionality.
